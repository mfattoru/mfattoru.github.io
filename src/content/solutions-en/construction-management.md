We provide comprehensive management services for the execution phase, serving as the bridge between design and physical realization. Our goal is to ensure that every project is built to the highest standards, respecting timelines, budgets, and safety regulations.

### 🏗️ Construction Supervision
We represent the client's interests on-site, ensuring constant oversight of quality and technical compliance.
* **Quality Control:** Continuous monitoring to ensure execution aligns with technical standards and project designs.
* **Regulatory Compliance:** Verification of adherence to building permits and current construction laws.
* **Contractor Auditing:** Technical assessment of the operational capabilities of construction firms and subcontractors.
* **Financial Management:** Precise drafting of site accounts and administrative verification of financial records and progress reports.

### 🛡️ Safety Coordination (CSP/CSE)
We manage the complexity of multi-contractor sites to protect worker health and mitigate legal liabilities for the property owner.
* **Design Phase (CSP):** Drafting the Safety and Coordination Plan (SCP) and conducting preliminary risk assessments.
* **Execution Phase (CSE):**
    * **Active Vigilance:** Constant on-site monitoring to enforce the application of safety protocols.
    * **Compliance Management:** Prompt reporting of irregularities and coordination with regulatory bodies (Health & Safety authorities).
    * **Technical Vetting:** Verification of the technical and professional suitability of every operator on-site.
    * **High-Risk Management:** Specific coordination for critical or hazardous work phases.
