We provide comprehensive support to businesses for managing safety in both fixed workplaces and construction sites. With deep expertise across all ATECO sectors and a specialized focus on the construction industry, we ensure maximum worker protection and full compliance with Legislative Decree 81/08.

### 🛡️ Management and Risk Assessment
* **External RSPP Services:** Assuming the role of Health and Safety Manager (RSPP) for all types of organizations.
* **DVR Drafting:** Analytical risk assessment and preparation of the Risk Assessment Document (DVR), with periodic updates reflecting regulatory and corporate changes.
* **Specialized Operating Procedures:** Definition of safety protocols for high-risk activities, including work in confined spaces, deep excavations, and operations in the presence of vehicular traffic.

### 📏 Instrumental Surveys and Diagnostics
We perform on-site technical surveys using certified instrumentation to monitor exposure to physical agents:
* **Environmental Analysis:** Phonometric (noise) and vibration surveys.
* **Health and Comfort:** Evaluation of lighting levels and microclimate conditions within the workplace.
* **Emergency Prevention:** Organization of evacuation drills, technical site inspections, and periodic safety audits.

### 🏗️ Site Safety and Documentation
We support contractors and coordinators in managing the technical documentation required by Title IV of the Italian Safety Act:
* **Technical Documentation:** Drafting of Safety and Coordination Plans (PSC), Operational Safety Plans (POS), Substitute Safety Plans (PSS), and Interference Risk Assessment Documents (DUVRI).
* **Subcontractor Vetting:** Verification of the technical and professional suitability of subcontractors.
* **Provisional Works:** Drafting of the Scaffolding Assembly, Use, and Dismantling Plan (Pi.M.U.S.) and relevant structural calculation reports where required.
