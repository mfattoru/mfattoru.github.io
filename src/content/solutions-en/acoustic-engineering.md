We provide specialized acoustic analysis and design services for both public and private clients, ensuring healthy, comfortable environments that are fully compliant with current regulations.

Our activities are organized into three main areas:

### 🏗️ Building and Architectural Acoustics
* **Passive Acoustic Requirements:** Design and on-site instrumental verification to ensure insulation from vibrations as well as impact, airborne, and equipment noise.
* **On-site Testing:** Certified phonometric testing to verify the acoustic performance of constructions and ensure full compliance with legal standards.
* **Acoustic Design & Comfort:** Optimization of the internal acoustic climate for complex environments such as recording studios, theaters, auditoriums, and classrooms, where sound quality is a fundamental parameter.

### 🌿 Environmental and Territorial Acoustics
* **Municipal Zoning Plans (P.Z.A.):** Drafting and updating urban planning tools for the management of the territorial acoustic climate.
* **Impact Assessments (E.I.A.):** Predictive and post-operam analyses for infrastructure, industrial activities, and residential developments.
* **Noise Mitigation:** Design of barriers and remediation interventions to manage noise pollution in urban and industrial settings.

### 📏 Surveys and Instrumental Diagnostics
* **Phonometric Monitoring:** Environmental and industrial noise measurement campaigns using high-precision instrumentation.
* **Diagnostic Investigations:** Detailed analyses to identify acoustic bridges or structural deficiencies and define targeted corrective interventions.
