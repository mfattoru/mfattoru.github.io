We provide professional appraisal services to objectively and accurately determine the value of real estate assets and land. Our expert technical reports support individuals and businesses during critical phases such as property transactions, inheritance, or legal disputes.

### 📊 Market Valuations and Estimates
* **Property Valuation:** Determination of the most probable market value for residential, commercial, and industrial properties, essential for purchase or sale operations.
* **Land & Development Sites:** Valuation of buildable land based on development potential, as well as appraisals for agricultural, wooded, or productive land.
* **Inheritance & Asset Division:** Technical consulting for the fair distribution of real estate assets within inheritance proceedings.

### ⚖️ Technical Appraisals & Damage Assessment
* **Damage Quantification:** Analytical evaluation and cost estimation of property damage resulting from construction defects, third-party conduct, or accidental events.
* **Condominium Disputes:** Technical reports for resolving disputes related to water leaks, boundary issues, or the use of common areas.
* **Certified Reports:** Drafting of sworn technical reports for legal, banking, or insurance purposes.
