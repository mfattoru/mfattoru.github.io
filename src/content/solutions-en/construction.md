We provide high-value technical consulting for private individuals, corporations, and construction firms. Our support covers the entire lifecycle of a building project, from tendering and procurement to operational management and long-term maintenance.

### 📋 Tender Management and Economic Planning
* **Tendering & Technical Proposals:** Comprehensive support in managing public procurement procedures and drafting technical value-added proposals (variant bids).
* **Bill of Quantities (BoQ) & Cost Estimation:** Accurate calculation of project costs to ensure reliable and transparent financial planning.
* **Technical Specifications:** Definition of technical documentation and performance requirements necessary for contract awarding.

### 🏗️ Technical and Operational Site Support
* **Material & Product Advisory:** Technical assistance in selecting products and construction technologies that align with specifications and industry standards.
* **Execution Verification:** Regular site visits to monitor the correct execution of works and ensure strict adherence to the original design.
* **Regulatory Compliance:** Ongoing information and support regarding technical and management obligations linked to on-site construction activities.

### ⚙️ Asset and Equipment Management
* **Maintenance Planning:** Development of maintenance programs for corporate equipment to ensure long-term efficiency and reliability.
* **Machinery Procurement:** Strategic support in selecting new machinery and instrumentation in full compliance with current regulations and directives.
