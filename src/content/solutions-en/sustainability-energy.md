The construction sector has a significant impact on our ecosystem. For this reason, we integrate sustainability criteria into every phase of design, aiming to reduce carbon footprints and optimize natural resources through a holistic and conscious approach.

### 📐 Bioclimatic and Sustainable Architecture
* **Bioclimatic Design:** Thorough analysis of local microclimates and building orientation to maximize natural comfort, drastically reducing energy demand for heating and cooling.
* **Life Cycle Assessment (LCA):** Selection of eco-compatible and recyclable materials, evaluated through LCA studies to ensure low environmental impact from production to disposal.
* **Innovative Materials:** Implementation of construction solutions featuring high thermal efficiency and low embodied energy.

### 🌿 Ecological Integration and Soil Protection
* **Urban Resilience:** Promoting soil permeability through the implementation of green roofs, ventilated facades, and sustainable drainage systems.
* **Natural Engineering:** Design of hydrogeological defense works against landslides and floods, utilizing low-impact techniques such as green walls, crib walls, and structural revegetation.
* **Biodiversity:** Integration of native plantings and biological technologies to restore the ecological balance of the site.

### ⚡ Renewable Energy and Certification
* **Renewable Energy Systems:** Design of integrated systems for clean energy production (photovoltaic, solar thermal, biomass boilers, and heat pumps).
* **Diagnostics and Certification:** Drafting of Energy Performance Certificates (EPC/APE) and consulting for access to tax incentives related to energy efficiency.
* **Consumption Monitoring:** Active energy control solutions to ensure maximum building performance over time.
